Method

Four stages.
No retainer mystery.

I.

Audit

Two weeks reviewing what's already running, before we propose a single new dollar of spend.

II.

Plan

A written roadmap with named channels, target numbers, and a date for the first checkpoint.

III.

Run

Campaigns launch. We adjust weekly against the data — not against the original plan for its own sake.

IV.

Report

A monthly review against the original targets. Underperformance gets named before you have to ask.
